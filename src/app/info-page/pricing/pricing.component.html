Initially, the price of each tile is fixed.

The central tile costs 1 ETH (or another coin, depending on the blockchain on which the smart contract is deployed).

The 4 tiles around the central one cost 0.25 ETH each.

The 8 tiles of the next circle are worth 0.125 ETH each.

The basic idea is that all tiles of one circle costs a total of 1 ETH. But at the same time a tile cannot cost less than 0.001 ETH.

Or if we speak in formula language:

price = MAX(1 / MAX(1, (ABS(x) + ABS(y)) * 4), 0.001)
